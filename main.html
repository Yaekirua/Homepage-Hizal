Story of Hizal Haziq

Hizal Haziq, or simply Hizal, was a 17-year-old wunderkind hailing from the picturesque landscapes of Malaysia. His life was a delightful blend of technological prowess and the simple pleasures of life.

From a young age, Hizal had been drawn to the allure of coding. The world of algorithms and lines of code fascinated him, and he spent countless hours exploring the intricacies of programming languages. It was a solitary journey that he relished, one that allowed him to tap into his creativity and problem-solving abilities. As he delved deeper into the world of technology, Hizal discovered his love for web development. He found joy in crafting websites that were not just functional but also aesthetically pleasing. Each project was an opportunity to blend his technical skills with his artistic sensibilities, resulting in beautifully designed online spaces. Hizal's educational journey led him to SMK Bandar Seri Putra, a school renowned for its focus on science and technology. Here, his coding talents flourished. He joined coding clubs and attended workshops to further refine his skills. His teachers recognized his potential and encouraged him to participate in coding competitions, where he consistently left a mark, earning accolades for his innovative solutions.
